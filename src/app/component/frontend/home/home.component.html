Meet “Coach Arnie”

Don’t limit your challenges, Challenge your limits!”

— Jerry Dunn

With a strong foundation in Education, I have been an educator, speaker, consultant, business owner and personality development coach, guiding countless people through physical, mental or emotional struggles in their personal, professional and social lives, successfully for close to the past four decades.

Apart from coaching individuals, couples and groups, as well as speaking at various events, I am also the founder of The Next Step Foundation — a 501c(3) organization that helps individuals, families — and other "Heart Centered" programs that aspire to my “Hands Of Hope" vision. My methods are aimed at empowering you to fight all adversities in each area of your life, and achieve success at very high levels.
